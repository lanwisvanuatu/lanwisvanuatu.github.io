Privacy Policy

SIL Vanuatu apps

Effective date: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile applications published by SIL Vanuatu ("we", "us", "our") on Google Play, including our Scripture apps and our educational apps for children. We care about your privacy and have designed our apps to collect as little information as possible.

In summary: our apps contain no advertising, require no account or sign-in, contain no in-app purchases, and do not collect any personally identifiable information. We use only anonymous usage statistics to help us improve our apps.

What access to your device does the app need?

Our apps may use your device's internet connection (to send the anonymous usage statistics described below) and may ask permission to show notifications (for example, a daily Scripture reminder or verse of the day). App content is stored on your device so that it can be used offline. These features run on your device and do not gather personal information about you.

What information might we collect?

When your device is connected to the internet, our apps send anonymous usage information to a Google Analytics for Firebase account that we use to understand how our apps are used. This information may include:

  • the type of device (for example, Samsung Galaxy);
  • the version of the operating system (for example, Android 13);
  • the app version and the general city or country where the device connects to the internet;
  • anonymous in-app activity, such as which screens or books are opened and how long the app is used.

This information is anonymous and aggregated. Our apps do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) — we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location, and our apps have no user accounts. We have also configured our analytics to be privacy-protective: advertising identifiers (the Android Advertising ID) are not collected, the "Google Signals" advertising feature is not enabled, and user-level data is kept for the shortest period offered (2 months). If your device is used offline, no usage information is sent.

What do we use this information for?

We use the anonymous usage statistics only to understand how many people use our apps and which parts are most used, so that we can fix problems and make the apps better. We do not use this information for advertising.

How do we protect the information we collect?

The anonymous usage information is sent over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection and is handled by Google's infrastructure on our behalf. Because our apps do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data about you for us to store or to lose.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer information to outside parties. The anonymous usage statistics are processed by Google as our service provider, as described above. We may disclose information when required to do so by law. For information about how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy and the Firebase privacy information at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Children's privacy

Some of our apps are directed to children, and we take children's privacy seriously. Our apps do not collect personal information from anyone, including children. They contain no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no account creation. For apps directed to children, our use of analytics follows Google Play's Families policy: advertising identifiers are not collected, and the data is used only for anonymous, aggregate measurement. We aim to comply with applicable children's privacy laws, including the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the children's provisions of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are a parent or guardian and have any question or concern, please contact us at the address below.

Your consent

By using our apps, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

If we change our Privacy Policy, we will post those changes on this page and update the effective date above.

Contacting us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our apps, you can contact us at apps_vanuatu@groups.sil.org.
